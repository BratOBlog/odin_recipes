Baked pumpkins

This Roasted Pumpkin recipe, serves up perfectly oven roasted pumpkin. It's an easy and versatile recipe, have them as pumpkin wedges with a dip or as a delicious side dish.

Ingredients

  • a fairly large and ripe pumpkin
  • a teaspoon of brown sugar for added sweetness (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cut pumpkin int half.
  2. Remove the seeds with a spoon.
  3. Lay out the pumpkins on a tray.
  4. Bake them until they are soft, to test poke the with a fork.
  5. YYou can add a bit of brown sugar on top for addedd sweetness.
