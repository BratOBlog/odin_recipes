Hungarian pancakes

What are pancakes?

Pancakes are made in a skillet and can be served as breakfeast or desert. Hungarian pancakes are flat and contain sparkling water for lighten up the pancakes. They are usually filled either with ground nuts, cocoa powder and icing sugar and sugar or apricot jam and rolled up instead of stacked."

Ingredients

  • 2 whole eggs
  • 1 1/4 cups all purpose flour
  • 1 1/4 cups whole milk
  • 2/3 cup sparkling water
  • 1 pinch salt
  • 1 teaspoon sugar
  • oil for frying

Instructions

  1. Mix ingredients in a bowl starting with the eggs.
  2. Add a pinch of salt, sugar and a bit of flour. Mix the batter until it is smooth like a yoghurt drink
  3. Fry the pancakes.
  4. Once done lay the pancake out and spread with the filling, after that roll it up.
  5. You can sprinkle with icing sugar before serving.
