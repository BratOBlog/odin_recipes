Lasagna

What is Lasagna

The Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made with ingredients layered over the top of one and other. Usually tomato flawored sauce, minced meat and lasagna noodels.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato product
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheese
  • Egg

How to make lasagna step by step

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the asagna.
  5. Cover with foil and bake.
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
